Pumpkin Soup

Description

A great recipe for a light dinner or an entrée.

This simple pumpkin soup is a staple in automn. The spices will keep you warm,

Ingredients

  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 tsp minced fresh ginger
  • 2 to 4 carrots, diced
  • 1/2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 large sweet potato
  • 1 medium pumkin, cut into chunks
  • greek yogurt for garnish

Steps

  1. « Toss the onion with the oil, garlic, ginger, and carrots.  »
  2. Add the curry powder, cayenne, cinnamon, nutmeg, and broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat, cover, and simmer for 10 to 15 minutes.
  3. Peel and cut the sweet potato into chunks. Add to the broth with the pumpkin. Cook for 3 to 5 minutes.
  4. Puree the soup in a food processor or blender in batches. Return to the pot, simmer, and add water to create a thinner consistency, if desired.
  5. Season to taste. Ladle into bowls and garnish with plain Greek yogurt.

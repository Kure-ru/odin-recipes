Chickpea Curry

Description

Chickpea curry is best served with rice or chapatis and a Yogurt Raita.

Save 1 1⁄2 cups of water that the chickpeas were cooked in to add to this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 cups dried chickpeas (garbanzo beans)
  • 11⁄2 cups reserved water (see note)
  • 2 tbsp good oil
  • 1⁄4 cup onions, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh ginger, minced
  • 1⁄4 tsp turmeric
  • 1⁄2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1⁄2 tsp ground coriander
  • 1⁄2 tsp garam masala
  • 1⁄4 cayenne pepper, or to taste
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1⁄4 cup fresh cilantro leaves, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Soak and cook chickpeas until they are done.  »
  2. In a medium pot on medium, heat oil. Add onions and sauté until they start to brown, 3–4 minutes.
  3. Add ginger, turmeric, cumin, coriander, garam masala, and cayenne, and sauté for another minute. Add chickpeas and reserved water.
  4. Bring liquid to a boil, then reduce heat to medium and simmer for about 20 minutes. When chickpeas are tender but not mushy, add lemon juice and adjust for salt.
  5. Serve garnished with cilantro.
